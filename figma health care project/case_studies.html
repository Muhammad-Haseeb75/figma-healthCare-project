Our Purpose

Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) is a structured, short-term, present-oriented psychotherapy that aims to change patterns of thinking or behavior.

Techniques & Approaches

CBT is based on the concept that your thoughts, feelings, physical sensations and actions are interconnected.

Results

CBT has been shown to be effective in treating anxiety, depression, and other mental health disorders.
